TESLA SCIENCE CENTER

  • Rebranding
  • Logo design
  • Merchandising

The centerpiece of the rebranding is the newly redesigned logo for the Tesla Science Center. The logo maintains the iconic essence of the original design while infusing it with a contemporary aesthetic. It features a sleek and minimalist representation of the center's name, emphasizing clean lines and a bold typography choice that exudes a sense of modernity and sophistication.

To enhance the scientific theme, the logo is surrounded by a mesmerizing animation of moving neutrons. These swirling particles symbolize the energy, movement, and constant evolution of scientific discovery. The dynamic nature of the neutrons adds a captivating element to the logo, capturing the attention and curiosity of viewers.

The rebranded logo is seamlessly integrated into various business materials, including letterheads, business cards, and email signatures.

As part of the rebranding efforts, a new trifold brochure has been created to showcase the Tesla Science Center's mission, research, and achievements. The brochure embraces the modernized logo as its focal point, featuring it prominently on the cover to establish brand recognition. The layout of the brochure is carefully designed to provide a clear and engaging presentation of information, with sections dedicated to the center's history, projects, and future initiatives. Vibrant imagery, concise copywriting, and a consistent design language ensure that the brochure effectively communicates the center's goals and values to its target audience.
