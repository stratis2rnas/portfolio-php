COVID-19 INFOGRAPHICS

  • Infographics
  • Illustration

The COVID-19 infographic project focuses on presenting statistical data related to the pandemic in Greece during the year 2020. The goal is to create an engaging and visually appealing design that effectively communicates key insights about the virus and its impact on the population.

The design incorporates various statistics, such as the average age of COVID-19 cases, the percentage of people who declined vaccination, and other relevant data points. The infographic utilizes clear and concise visual representations, including charts, graphs, and icons, to present these statistics in an easily digestible format.

To enhance visual impact, a vibrant color palette featuring neon pink and blue is employed throughout the infographic. These colors add a modern and attention-grabbing element to the design, making it visually appealing and enticing for viewers.
