COFFEE BEANS

  • Product design
  • Branding/Identity
  • Illustration

The Psychedelic Coffee project is a dynamic and visually engaging exploration of the intersection between coffee culture and psychedelic art. Drawing inspiration from the vibrant colors and bold patterns associated with psychedelic design, this project offers a fresh and unconventional take on traditional coffee packaging.

The goal was to create a branding and packaging design that would capture the essence of both coffee and psychedelic art, creating a unique and visually stunning brand.

Through careful research and creative experimentation, I developed a unique and cohesive brand identity that captures the essence of the coffee and the psychedelic aesthetic. The packaging design features bold, swirling patterns in vibrant hues of pink, purple, and blue, creating an eye-catching and memorable visual impact.

In addition to the packaging design, the project includes a range of mockups that showcase how the brand identity can be applied across a variety of mediums, including coffee cups and posters
