KALLISTI WINE

  • Product design
  • Branding/Identity
  • Illustration

The Kallisti Wine project was a passion project for me, and I am honored to have had the opportunity to work on such a beautiful brand. As a solo designer, I was inspired by the unique blend of nature and winemaking found on the Greek island of Samos.


The concept behind the project was to create a wine label design that combines the elegant and luxurious feel of the wine with elements inspired by the island's nature.


With a deep respect for the island's heritage and culture, I carefully crafted a custom logo and packaging design that captured the essence of Samos. The label design features elegant typography and a custom logo that perfectly embodies the rugged terrain and coastal waters of the island. Throughout the project, I paid close attention to every detail, ensuring that each aspect of the design was thoughtfully considered. From the use of high-quality materials to the stunning color palette, I strived to create a design that was both beautiful and functional.


I am proud of the results of this project, and I believe that it showcases my ability to create visually stunning branding solutions that are both authentic and effective. I hope that this project inspires others to explore the beauty of Samos and the unique qualities of its winemaking traditions. In sharing this project with potential clients, I hope to demonstrate my commitment to delivering exceptional designs that exceed expectations and elevate brands to new heights.
